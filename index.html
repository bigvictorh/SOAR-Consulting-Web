About Us

Hypothermia Devices, Inc. is bringing non-invasive cooling into the digital age.

Hypothermia Devices designs and manufactures state-of-the-art cooling/heating devices for a wide range of medical applications, including therapeutic hypothermia. These systems are compact, portable, battery operated, and work at room temperature. Their unique features make them suitable as emergency equipment in ambulances, as well as ideal replacements for bulky hypothermia equipment currently taking up valuable real estate in hospitals and other medical facilities.

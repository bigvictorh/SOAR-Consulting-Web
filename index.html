About Us

As a team of six UCLA students, we launched SOAR Consulting in late 2015 with one simple purpose: to help students and their organizations succeed. Our founding team members have all held leadership roles in various campus organizations and bring unique backgrounds and interests to the table – from consulting to pre-health to engineering.

SOAR Consulting offers pro-bono consulting services for undergraduate organizations including but not limited to strategic planning for marketing campaigns, recruiting, public affairs, system restructuring, constitutional restructuring, and on-campus event planning. We are a by students for students service and aim to both improve the quality of undergraduate organization work and provide an opportunity for students within SOAR Consulting to gain a multitude of consulting experience in a wide variety of organization types.

We believe our collective, extensive expertise and knowledge will allow us to solve any issues your organization may be facing and implement lasting change to ensure a bright future.

Recruitment

We started SOAR Consulting because we wanted to leverage our collective experiences and expertise to help students at UCLA with their different organizations. Immediately, we began advising clients on a variety of cases and the feedback we’ve received has been extremely positive.

Now as we look to increase our reach and influence, we are opening up recruitment for SOAR Consultants to join our team. You will gain immediate hands-on consulting and leadership experience while leaving a positive impact in the UCLA community.

No consulting experience is required, and we will provide onboard training to ensure everyone starts off on the right foot.

To apply, please send your resume and a brief statement (1-2 paragraphs) outlining your interest in SOAR Consulting to soarconsultingucla@gmail.com. If you have any questions, please feel free to reach out to us.
